Accessing the JEDI Archives

Join JEDI   View JEDI Archives

The Journal Editors Discussion Interface has an archive of all messages on Google Groups. As a JEDI member, you can access the archive here. Your e-mail has to be associated with a google account to allow access. Contact the JEDI administrator if you have trouble accessing the archive.

If you are not yet a member, join JEDI here.
